# Project Overview
Name: THE SPOT

Brief History:
THE SPOT was founded in 2023 by K, a visual artist who recognised art as evidence of the mind’s interior journey. The organisation emerged from a collection of works by artists who create as a means of traversing inner terrain

Mission:
 To empower artists by providing a platform that showcases creative expression as evidence of the mind’s journey, invites visitors to explore the boundless dimensions of human consciousness, and offers, for those who seek it, education and advocacy at the intersection of art and mental health.

Vision:
A world where every mind finds witness, where art is recognised as both archive and experience, and where those who wish to understand more deeply find resources waiting.


Target Audience:
•	Artists (emerging and established) seeking a non-commercial platform
•	Art enthusiasts
•	Mental health advocates and organisations
•	Individuals exploring mental health
•	Students, educators, and researchers

The Realms:
Each realm within THE SPOT represents a distinct artistic medium and serves as a gateway into the different expression of the mind’s interior landscape.

# Website Goals and Objectives
1. Create a platform where artists can submit work that documents 	their inner experience
2. Design and develop a dimensional realm structure  that encourages visitors to explore multiple art styles
3. Foster a growing community around the organisation’s mission
5. Position the spot as a recognised voice in the South African arts and mental health
landscape
6. Develop a section containing curated resources
7. Develop a website that meets  internationally recognised
benchmarks 	for
accessible web design. It should also achieve a
relatively high mobile friendly score
