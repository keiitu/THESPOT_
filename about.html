The Spot

THE SPOT was founded in 2023 by K, a visual artist and curator whose practice has long explored the relationship between creative expression, and the inner experience. His philosophy is a belief that: art is an experience, a manefestation of the mind's boundless capacity to traverse the unknown. Each mark on a page, and every stroke of line drawn mapped evidence of this interior journey, a record of having ventured into some previously unchartered region of thought or feeling.


What We Do

  • Showcase a wide range of artworks, classified by art style or "realm"
  • Provide resources and opportunities for emerging and established artists
  • Promote art education and appreciation through engaging content
  • Facilitate connections between artists, collectors, and enthusiats

Our Mission

To empower artists by providing a platform that showcases as evidence of the mind's journey, invites visitors to explore the boundless dimensions of human consciousness, and offers education and advocacy at the intersection of art and mental health.


Our Vision

A world where finds witness, where art is recognised as both archive and experience, and where those who wish to understand more deeply find resources waiting.
